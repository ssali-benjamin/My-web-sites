About Our Organization

Hope Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives through humanitarian aid, sustainable development, and advocacy.

Founded in 2010, we've helped over 50,000 people across 15 countries through our various programs and initiatives.

  • 100% transparent operations
  • 90% of donations go directly to programs
  • Award-winning organization
  • Registered 501(c)(3) non-profit
